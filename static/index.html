A simple API for bus service timings at Temasek Polytechnic

Read the full documentation @ https://github.com/samuelyapwoonpin/tpbus/blob/master/README.md
Get timings of bus services at all bus stops
Definition: /services
Example: tpbus.herokuapp.com/services
Get timings of bus services at specified bus stop
Definition: /services/<busStopLocation>
Example: tpbus.herokuapp.com/services/west-gate (Gets the timings of the bus services at TP's west gate)
Get timings of specified bus service at specified bus stop
Definition: /services/<busStopLocation>/<serviceNumber>
Example: tpbus.herokuapp.com/services/main-gate/23 (Gets the timings of the bus 23 at TP's main gate)
